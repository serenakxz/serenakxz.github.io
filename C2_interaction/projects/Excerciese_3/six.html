Tonight, there is going to a big storm on the sea. At first, I was next to a large ship. On the ship beautiful music was playing. A handsome young man stepped out on the deck. “He must be a prince.”, I said. Suddenly, it became very dark and the wind picked up. Then lightning. Thunder. A strong rainstorm hit. The poor ship started to tip on the rough waves! I must save the people on the boat. I swim towards the boat.
